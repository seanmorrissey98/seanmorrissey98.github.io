What can I
expect during a treatment?

Your initial session will begin in a private warm and friendly
environment with a review of your health history and the issues you wish to
resolve.

During the
therapy session you will lie comfortably and fully clothed on a treatment
table. You will be given open toed shoes with a flat heel to wear during the
session, This enables easy identification of foot-leg movements when held by
the therapist during the bioresonance scan.

This leg
length imbalance enables the therapist to identify areas of the body that
indicate the prescence of harmful pathogens, dysfunctions (organ, glandular and
hormonal) and other issues which are then treated by placing and taping pairs
of magnets on specific locations on the body to restore balance.

When the body
has been fully scanned the magnets are left on for approx 20 minutes. The
entire session takes around 1 1/2 hours for the initial consultation and about
an hour for follow up sessions.

Most of our clients
feel relaxed during the process while some report feeling a tingling sensation
or a vibration.

As the
session concludes we remove the magnets and give you some feedback about what
has shown up during your treatment. We can then schedule follow up appointments
as needed and at follow up appointments we can review any changes to your
health since your last visit.
