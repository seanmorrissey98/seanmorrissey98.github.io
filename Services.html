01 · Purpose

Purpose & objectives.

The purpose of this therapeutic discipline is to bring biomagnetic balance to the organs, tissues and areas of the body that are out of alignment — both with the body's own magnetic field and with the earth's magnetic field. And to balance all of the microbes in the body: viruses, bacteria, fungi and parasites.

Microbes make up around 85% of the DNA in the body. When they are unwell or out of balance, they tend to cause illness. Balanced microbes, on the other hand, contribute to healthy biological function and play a vital role in the body's immune and defence system.

02 · Benefits

A gentle, drug-free practice.

Biomagnetic Therapy is a safe, gentle and very effective therapy with practically no side effects — and it's completely compatible with other traditional and alternative practices.

One of the things I value most is that I can tune in to the person themselves to get the answers, rather than relying on symptoms alone. Through bioresonance scanning, the person's own body tells me what needs to be balanced to bring them back to full health.

Conventional medicine tends to target the symptoms or degenerative signs caused by a single pathogen, and doesn't always address the additional pathogens that are part of the wider disease process. This therapy is used to find the origin of a person's problem — even when the source hasn't yet developed any symptoms — making it preventative for the complex, chronic, degenerative diseases, syndromes and tumours that negatively affect a person's health.

So our goal for every client is to bring balance — coherence — to all the microbes, organs, tissues and areas of the body that are out of biomagnetic balance, and to restore your natural healing ability.

What to expect

Your treatment, step by step.

From the moment you arrive to the conversation at the end of your session.

  1. A warm welcome

    Your initial session begins in a private, warm and friendly environment with a review of your health history and the issues you'd like to resolve.

  2. Settling in

    You'll lie comfortably and fully clothed on a treatment table, wearing the open-toed flat shoes we provide so foot and leg movements can be observed during the scan.

  3. The bioresonance scan

    Through subtle leg-length imbalances, I identify areas of the body indicating harmful pathogens, dysfunctions (organ, glandular, hormonal) and other issues that need attention.

  4. Restoring balance

    Pairs of magnets are placed and taped on specific locations on the body to restore balance, and stay in place for around 20 minutes. Many clients feel deeply relaxed; some report a gentle tingling or vibration.

  5. Feedback & follow-up

    Once we remove the magnets I'll share what came up during your treatment, and we can schedule any follow-ups you'd like.

  6. Time to allow

    Initial consultations take around 1½ hours. Follow-up sessions take about an hour, and at each one we'll review the changes in your health since your last visit.

Cancellation policy

I understand cancellations are sometimes unavoidable — please give 24 hours' notice so that your appointment can be offered to someone else, otherwise you will be invoiced for the cost of the session. If you fail to attend without notice, the cancellation fee is 100% of your treatment cost.
