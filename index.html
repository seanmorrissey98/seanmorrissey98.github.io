About Me

Welcome to my site!

My name is Sean Morrissey and I am a 19 year old first year student in the University Of Limerick. I am currently studying Computer Systems and I am having a lot of fun doing so! The constant challenges and innovation requirements of the course are allowing me to enjoy my work and thus improve my skills.

So far I have gained valuable knowledge on various modules such as: Discrete Mathematics, Computer Maths, Computer Science, Software Development, Games Development, Model-Driven Development, Computer Organisations, Databases, Operating Systems, OOP and Software Testing and Requirements.

In my time in college so far I have completed many projects and assignments be it solo or in teams. Some of which can be viewed on my Projects page or on my Github. In some of these projects such as my League Manager application I have taken on the role of project leader, allowing me to improving various skills of mine such as delegation and organisation.

Outside of my course and college i am an avid fan of sports, music, gaming and socializing. I am a member of various clubs and societies such as Social Soccer but my main love is for computing, so every spare minute I get is spent on furthering my knowledge or working on projects.
